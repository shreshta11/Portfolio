Autonomous Rescue Operations Robot

Aug 2024–Dec 2024 — under Prof. Necmiye Ozay

Highlights

  • Sensor fusion for environmental mapping; robust serial/IPC between subsystems.
  • Closed-loop motor control tuned for variable payloads and terrain.

Tech

C/C++, BeagleBone Blue, Arduino Nano, PWM motor control, I²C/SPI/UART.
