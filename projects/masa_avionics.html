Power Distribution Board — MASA “Limelight”

Avionics Project Lead & Outreach Director — Dec 2023–Present

Designed and validated a high-reliability PDB enabling seamless ground→flight battery transition. Performed switching tests and troubleshooting during integration; acted as Console Operator during cold-flow tests.

Highlights

  • Schematic + PCB in Altium; isolation, fusing, and brown-out resilience.
  • Oscilloscope-based validation under dynamic loads; mitigation of transients.
  • Procedural checklists for GSE control during pre-launch sequences.

Tech

Altium Designer, oscilloscopes/DMMs, high-side switching, DC-DC regulation.
