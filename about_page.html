About

Hi! I’m Shreshta, an electrical engineering student at the University of Michigan focused on hardware systems, power electronics, and compute architectures. I love building: from avionics for student rocketry, to autonomous robots, to PIM‑NoC accelerators for efficient Transformer inference.

Recently, I interned with the SEEDER Group at the National University of Singapore, where I explored hardware–software co‑design for memory‑centric accelerators. Before that, I worked on power systems at Quest Global.

In college, you’ll find me working on avionics or organizing outreach with MASA, the student rocketry team at the University of Michigan, performing Indian Classical Music with Michigan Sahana, or playing basketball with my friends.
